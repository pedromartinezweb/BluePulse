# Privacy Policy

BluePulse does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

## Data collection

BluePulse does not collect:

- Personal information.
- Analytics.
- Telemetry.
- Location data.
- Device identifiers.
- Usage history.

## Network access

BluePulse does not require internet access and does not send network requests.

## Local settings

BluePulse may store local application settings on the user's Windows profile, such as whether the utility is enabled and the configured idle threshold. These settings stay on the local device.
